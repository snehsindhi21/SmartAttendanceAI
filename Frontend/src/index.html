How It Works

Seamless integration from login to attendance marking.

01

Authentication

User logs in securely via our JWT-based portal, ensuring role-based access for Students and Faculty with encrypted sessions.

02

Biometric Scan

The system activates the camera to capture facial data and verifies liveness (blinking/movement) to prevent photo spoofing.

03

AI Validation

Captured face embeddings are compared against the encrypted vector database using high-speed deep learning models.

04

Success

Attendance is marked instantly in the secure database, and a real-time confirmation toast is displayed to the user.

Advanced Features

Our system uses cutting-edge Deep Learning models to ensure reliability, security, and ease of use for educational institutions.

Security

Liveness Detection

Prevents fraud by ensuring the person is physically present. The system detects micro-movements, depth, and texture to reject photos or video screens.

Location

Geofencing

Restricts attendance marking to specific classroom coordinates only. Students must be within the virtual perimeter to mark presence, ensuring physical attendance.

Analytics

Smart Analytics

Generate automated weekly and monthly reports for faculty in one click. Identify attendance trends and at-risk students instantly with visual graphs.

Mobile

Cross-Platform

Accessible from any device – Mobile, Tablet, or Desktop via web browser (PWA supported). No specialized hardware required, just a standard camera.
